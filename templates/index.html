MarketGPT

MarketGPT is an AI-powered financial news analysis platform that aggregates, analyzes, and summarizes market news to provide actionable insights – all powered by your local or cloud-based LLM.

💬 Try the Chat

✨ Features

📰 Automated News Aggregation

Pulls breaking news from financial RSS feeds like CNBC and stores them for analysis.

🤖 Configurable LLM Pipeline

Use OpenAI, Claude, or a local model (LM Studio, Ollama) with an easy JSON config.

📚 Semantic Vector Search

Query news by meaning, not just keywords – powered by ChromaDB + embeddings.

📈 AI Stock Recommendations

Extracts BUY/SELL signals from headlines and explains the reasoning and source.

📊 Market Report Generator

Automatically classifies news into themes and creates daily market summaries.

💬 Chat Q&A Interface

Ask questions about today’s news and get answers sourced from the actual articles.
